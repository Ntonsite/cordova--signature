FAQS
  • I can not log in the App?

    Make sure your data is on. Use your current domain password to log in the app.

  • What happens if my application is not submitted?

    When the application fails to be submitted it is stored in archives. Please go to that page and click on the specific application and follow the neccessary steps.

  • At what time can I submit an account?

    An account can be submitted at any time. All applications subimitted during the work hours will be opened on the same day.

    All Applications submitted after working hours will be opened on the next day.
